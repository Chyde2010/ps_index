## Why the prediction was wrong

The positive prediction was based on a C7 lean pass -- the
connector ecosystem compounding switching cost moat argument.
This was incorrect for two reasons documented post-pipeline:

1. Competitive pressure: Klaviyo listed NYSE Sep 2023 at
   $9bn valuation -- 4x DOTD's entire market cap. Braze and
   Mailchimp also built equivalent connector ecosystems.
   DOTD's engineering was maintaining competitive parity
   not compounding an insurmountable moat.

2. Peak investment = competitive response, not moat compounding:
   The 2021 HC cluster (highest z-scores in study) coincided
   with the beginning of DOTD's 75-80% de-rating from peak.
   Peak engineering intensity was a response to competitive
   pressure, not evidence of compounding advantage.
   The stock fell 65-80% in the 6M following peak HC episodes.

This is the same mechanism as MNDY:
- Company priced for growth premium at peak
- High engineering intensity as response to competition
- Severe de-rating as growth slowed and multiples compressed
- Signal correctly detected the inflection point
